# Code of conduct

Koru welcomes contributors and users with different backgrounds, identities, experiences, and opinions. Participation must remain safe, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy; never post another person's writing, clipboard content, credentials, or personal information.
- Assume good intent while responding to actual impact.
- Accept maintainer decisions and continue disagreement without harassment.
- Help make technical and product discussions understandable to newcomers.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized content, doxxing, sustained disruption, impersonation, and publishing private security reports or user content are not accepted.

## Enforcement

Use GitHub's repository reporting tools to report conduct concerns privately. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, restrict participation, or ban accounts when necessary. Reports are handled as privately as the platform allows, and retaliation against a reporter is itself a violation.

This policy applies in project repositories, discussions, issue trackers, reviews, community spaces, and public representation of the project.
